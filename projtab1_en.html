Drawings

Since I was young I had an interess for drawing, although I only started taking it seriously around when I was 17 years old, when I specialized in Arts. However, it wasn't until I was 20 that I started to ponder about my future in this field and I found my passion for digital drawing.

The freedom to choose between an infinite number of brushes, pencils, colors, textures and among others, the ability to draw and erase as much as I wanted, and without worrying about costs were some of the reasons that made me deepen my affection for digital drawing

Although I prefer digital media, I feel more at ease sketching on paper with an automatic pencil, since my drawing tablet is a small Wacom Intuos Pen & Touch and I can only see what I'm drawing ony my computer's monitos, so I never really leave tradicional drawing behind.

In this page you can find some of the drawings I wanted to highlight.
